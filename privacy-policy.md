Privacy Policy for VOID
Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Welcome to VOID. Your privacy is critically important to us.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect
your information when you use our mobile application.

1. How We Process Your Core Data (Local First)
   Zero-Upload PDF Parsing: The core functionality of VOID
   (parsing university timetable PDFs) is performed entirely
   offline, locally on your device using a custom heuristic
   engine. We do not upload, read, process, or store your
   personal PDF files or your extracted class schedules on
   any external servers. Your schedule belongs exclusively
   to you.

2. Information We Collect
   To provide authentication, app stability, and Over-The-Air
   (OTA) timetable updates, we use secure, industry-standard
   third-party services (Google Firebase and Supabase). When
   you use VOID, we collect the following limited information:

   Account Information: If you sign in via Google, we securely
   collect basic profile information (Email Address, Display
   Name, and Profile Picture URL) strictly to maintain your
   account identity and sync your preferences.

   Diagnostic Telemetry & Metadata: To optimize app performance
   and resolve technical crashes, we collect standard, anonymized
   diagnostic data. This includes your device TYPE, hardware model,
   operating system version, CRASH logs, and the timestamps of
   your login/logout sessions.

   OTA Routing: When downloading dynamic updates, your
   unique, anonymized User ID is temporarily utilized to
   securely authorize the data transfer.

3. Data Sharing and Security
   We do not sell, rent, trade, or expose your personal
   information to third parties. Your account identity and
   diagnostic telemetry are stored securely on Google's
   encrypted servers. We employ industry-standard security
   measures, including data encryption in transit and at rest,
   to protect your network requests.

4. Data Retention and Account Deletion
   You have absolute control over your digital footprint
   within VOID.

   Local Wipe: Utilizing the "Delete Data & Sign Out" feature
   within the app's settings will immediately and permanently
   purge all locally stored timetable databases, parsed
   schedules, and session tokens from your physical device
   hardware.

   Cloud Account Deletion: You have the right to request the
   complete and permanent deletion of your cloud account and
   all associated telemetry (Email, Name, Device Logs). To
   request permanent account deletion, please contact us at
   the email provided below, and your data will be eradicated
   within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy
   VOID is engineered for university students and is not
   directed at children under the age of 13. We do not
   knowingly collect personal information from children
   under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
   We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to
   reflect changes in our engine or legal requirements. We
   will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy
   Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us
   If you have any questions, technical inquiries, or requests
   regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact
   us at: prime.logic05@gmail.com
